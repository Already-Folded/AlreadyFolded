FAQ

Questions from directors

Is there any cost to the daycare to partner?+
No. Partnership is completely free. We provide all bags, onboarding materials, and ongoing account support. Daycares earn revenue — they don't spend any.
What exactly does our staff have to do?+
Accept a labeled bag from a parent at drop-off, store it briefly, hand it to our driver when they collect — then return the clean bag at pickup. We estimate under 5 minutes per day total.
How does the referral revenue get paid?+
Monthly, automatically, via direct deposit or check. You receive a summary showing participating families and commission earned. No invoicing required on your end.
We operate multiple locations. Does this scale?+
Yes. Multi-site operators get a single agreement, centralized billing, one account manager, and consolidated monthly reporting. Revenue compounds quickly across locations.
What if a parent has a complaint about their laundry?+
All service issues are handled directly by Already Folded. Parents contact us — not your staff. If anything is unsatisfactory, we re-wash it at no charge, guaranteed.
Is there a minimum enrollment size to partner?+
We work with daycares of all sizes. Smaller centers may see lower initial adoption, but the service still adds real value as an enrollment differentiator — and revenue grows as families join.
