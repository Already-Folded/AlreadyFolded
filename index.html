Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the wash and fold delivery service cost?

Pricing is based on weight and your specific daycare's partnership plan. Please contact us or check with your daycare administrator for current rates in your service area.

Is my child's laundry washed separately?

Absolutely. Every order is cleaned, dried, and folded in its own dedicated load using premium, hypoallergenic detergents, guaranteeing safety and quality. **Consistency is guaranteed.**

How does the partnership with my daycare work?

We provide all necessary supplies, training, and a dedicated account manager. Your staff only needs to coordinate the drop-off and pickup times—zero washing or folding is required by your team.

Do I need to sign up if my daycare is already a partner?

Yes. Enrollment is quick and easy through a private link provided by your daycare. This ensures your laundry is tagged and handled correctly from day one.
