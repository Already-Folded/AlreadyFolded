Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Already Folded (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

  • Partnership Inquiries: Name, email, daycare name, and enrollment size provided via our contact forms.
  • Parent Information: Names and contact details for those participating in the wash & fold program.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to facilitate laundry services, manage partnership revenue, and communicate service updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption to protect lead and customer information. Payment processing is handled by secure, PCI-compliant third-party providers.
